FRIDA KAHLO

1907 - 1954

i leave you my portrait so that you will have my presence all the days and nights that i am away from you

Name + Origin

Frida's full name is Magdalena Carmen Frida Kahlo y Calderón and she was born July 6, 1907 in Coyoacán, Mexico, in her house La Casa Azul.

Unfortunate Beginnings

Frida contracted polio at age 6 and suffered from a severe bus accident, thus causing her to be bedridden and infertile.

The Unconventional Artist

After her accident is when Frida started pursue art more seriously. Laying in bed with a mirror on her ceiling, Frida painted away her both physiological and psychological pain.

Diego + Love

Frida married Diego Rivera, a popular muralist of her time at 22, while he was 42. A lot of Frida's art and personal style was inspired by their love - when the relationship went well, she reflected in bright colors; and when the relationship faced infidelity, divorce, remarriage, and everything in between, her art reflected that.

More Than Just A Reflection

Frida produced over 151 paintings in her lifetime, 55 being self-portraits. She always said that she painted herself because she "was the subject she knew best".

Death and The Afterlife

Frida passed away on July 13th, 1954 at age 47. Her last diary entry read, "I hope the end is joyful - and I hope to never return. - Frida".
